About

A self-taught programmer started coding way back in 2010. Was introduced to algorithms and coding in 10th class as a part of course curriculum. My interest grew up quickly and started coding daily, initially in C++ language. And here I am 6 years later, completing multiple projects, internships, competitive coding challenges, hackathons, etc. Wins and losses all came down the line but my love for programming never changed.
